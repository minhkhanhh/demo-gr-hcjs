About Us

Our Story

A world where Vietnamese coffee is enjoyed and appreciated by many people.

Operating with the vision of becoming a leading player in the coffee industry, we aim to contribute to enhancing the brand and role of Vietnamese coffee in the global supply chain. After a long journey of continuous development, Tsunami Coffee has become an important partner of hundreds of distribution agents; Coffee shops at home and abroad such as the US, Germany, Russia, Korea, China… We builds its business on the aspiration of a sustainable coffee community; make practical contributions to the development of people and society.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading company in Vietnam in supplying, processing and exporting high quality coffee. We want to bring Vietnamese coffee to the world, affirming the position of the world's second largest coffee exporter. The message “Miracle to wake up” (“Switch you”) is always hidden behind every cup of Detech coffee: awakening love, creativity and social responsibility… Those values continue to be connected and spread. radiate. strong through each cup of Detech coffee every day towards a more prosperous Vietnam.
